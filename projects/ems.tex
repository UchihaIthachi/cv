    \item Authored production-grade \textbf{Kubernetes} manifests (Deployments, StatefulSets, Ingress, PVCs), configured \textbf{HPA} for auto-scaling, secured configs/secrets, and integrated with \textbf{OCI Load Balancer}.
    \item Automated builds and tests with \textbf{GitHub Actions}; deployed with \textbf{ArgoCD GitOps} for sync, rollback, and drift detection.
\end{itemize}
